About Us

What's so great about a mathematician from the MIddle Ages? Fibonacci created the coolest numbes in history, where each one equals the sum of the two numbers before it (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 - you gte it). A thousand years since his death, people have noticed this sequence in nature, applied it in business and architecture, and created music by it's pattern. We think that's pretty cool!

For the last 3 years Fibonacci's has served a sequence of good food to anyone who enjoys a nerdy pum. Now we are expaning to bring you a food truk! Enjoy Fibonacci;s att local events or book us for a neighborhood visit or special ocassion.

Fibonacci's is owned by Paula Delfino and inspired by her father Raul, a retired teacher who taught with a great knowledge in the classroom and a wacky sense of humor everywhere else.
